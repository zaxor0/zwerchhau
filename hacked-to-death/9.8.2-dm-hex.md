# Hex Generation
for DMs

## Travel & Hex Procedures
1. Once party rolls weather, roll 1d4 for additional descriptions
2. Next determine events, also places if you are freshly generating this hex.

## Weather Descriptions 
Per the weather roll from the **Hex Crawl Procedures**, roll on these tables for more detail and flavor.

| **Snow**                    | **Heavy Snow**                 |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------|
| A gentle snowfall           | Heaps of snow, slowing travel  |
| Billowing, low visibility   | Icey crust and rime            |

## Events and Places 
- Something should always happen in a hex, but it doesn't always have to lead to combat.
- Encounters should be events, and not necessarily be tied to a given location.
- **Places**, each hex should have something unique in it, use this to help flavor the encounter
- Roll 1d6 for unweighted events, or 2d6 for weighted events
- For the **Dungeon** place, see the mythic underworld section or use a preprepared dungeon.

| Roll    | Events          | Places       |
|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| 6 or 7  | Encounter       | Natural      |
| 5 or 8  | Wildlife        | Lair         |
| 4 or 9  | Hazard          | Ruin         |
| 3 or 10 | Faction         | Dungeon      |
| 2 or 11 | Large encounter | Soceity      |
| 1 or 12 | Dragon          | Evil Wizard  |
